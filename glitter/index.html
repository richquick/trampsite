Glitter in the Dark

2020 is a tough year to be an actor, and an even tougher time to start your acting career.

So we wanted to shine some light on the glittering talent who are starting their careers while theatres are dark - and to provide them with a chance to showcase themselves.

We're giving one 2020 graduate, or other actor coming into the industry at this difficult time, the chance to star in the World Premiere of a brand new Philip Ridley monologue, which will form part of our The Beast Will Rise season.

You'll star alongside actors like Rachel Bright (Eastenders), Unique Spencer (Top Boy), Mirren Mack (The Nest, Sex Education), Steve Furst (Matilda, St Trinians, Little Britain), Lucy Gape (Hollyaoks), and Charlie Quirke (Birds of a Feather).

Here's a taster of the monologues so far..

What will I get?

As well as the chance to star in a Philip Ridley world premiere, we're offering lots of other things help you kickstart your career, including mentoring sessions, a copy of your world premiere for your showreel, a Spotlight credit for 2020, a small cash prize, and more.

Can I enter?

The competition is open to 2020 graduates and other actors coming into the industry at this difficult time. See the FAQ for more information about entry requirements.

How to enter

To enter, please record 1-2 minutes from any of the Philip Ridley monologues that have been part of The Beast Will Rise season and submit your video here.

We’ll shortlist our favourites, with the help of a panel of industry professionals. The winner will get to work with director Wiebke Green on a never-before-seen monologue which will be shown on our website and YouTube channel to a worldwide audience.

Questions? We’re sure you’ve got loads .. so please check our FAQ, and if the answer isn’t there, ask us on Twitter.
