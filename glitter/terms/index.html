Glitter in the Dark Competition

Terms & Conditions

  1. The Glitter in the Dark Prize is open to actors leaving training in 2020.
  2. The competition is open to entrants aged 16 or over. There is no upper age limit.
  3. There is no restriction on where entrants are from.
  4. Entrants should select a 1-2 minute section from any of the monologues in The Beast Will Rise season to perform.
  5. Entries should be received by 5pm on Monday 29th June 2020. Any entries received after this time will not be considered.
  6. The finalists will be announced on Saturday 11th July.
  7. The winner will be announced on Monday 13th July, and have their monologue shown on the Tramp website, YouTube, and Twitter channels on 18th July.
  8. Entrants should sumbit a video of no more than 2 minutes via the submission form.
  9. The monologue should be performed in English or in sign language.
  10. There is no entry fee and no purchase necessary to enter this competition.
