Glitter in the Dark Competition

Terms & Conditions

  1. The Glitter in the Dark Prize is open to emerging actors who have graduated within the past 2 years from full time acting training, as well as other emerging actors at the start of their career or still in training.
  2. There is no upper or lower age limit on entrants.
  3. There is no restriction on where entrants are from.
  4. Entrants should select a 1-2 minute section from any of the monologues in The Beast Will Rise season to perform.
  5. Entries should be received by 1pm on Tuesday 23rd of June 2020. Any entries received after this time will not be considered.
  6. The finalists will be announced on Monday 29th June. Finalists must be available for rehearsals during the week of 29th June.
  7. Entrants should sumbit a video of no more than 2 minutes via the submission form.
  8. The monologue should be performed in English or in sign language.
  9. There is no entry fee and no purchase necessary to enter this competition.
  10. Entrants may submit more than one entry as long as each entry is from a different monologue.
  11. Tramp will share all submissions on Social Media and YouTube, and will provide closed captions on YouTube.
  12. Entrants are encouraged to upload and share their monologues on social media, crediting Philip Ridley as the writer.
  13. Copyright of the monologues and performances remains at all times with Philip Ridley.
  14. The finalists will be send their edited, colour graded versions of the world premiere monologue and shall have permission to use on their showreel.
  15. By entering this competition, an entrant is indicating his/her agreement to be bound by these terms and conditions.
  16. No cash alternative to the prizes will be offered. The prizes are not transferable. We reserve the right to substitute any prize with another of equivalent value without giving notice.
