Eleanor Fanyinka

Eleanor Fanyinka is an actor from London.

She trained at LAMDA and is perhaps best known for her role as Morven Digby on BBC's Holby City.

Theatre includes: Leave A Message, (Gilded balloon Edinburgh) Losing Venice (Orange tree theatre) Sweeney Todd (Manchester Royal Exchange and West Yorkshire Playhouse) Hidden (Oval House) 24 hour plays (old vic new voices) Comedy of Errors (Manchester royal exchange) In The Blood (Finborough theatre) Harold Pinter A Celebration (National Theatre).

Tv includes: Shakespeare & Hathaway (BBC) Midsomer Murders (ITV) Pure (channel 4) Holby City (BBC).

Feature length film includes: Tom and Jerry, (Warner Bros), Masters Of Love (Truck Films).

Short Films: Team Player, Second Skin.
