Nancy Sullivan

Nancy is an award-winning working class actor, teacher, writer, and director from Bermondsey.

She won Fringe First & The Stage Edinburgh Awards for her lead role in Fabric, which transferred to Soho Theatre, played Eponine in Les Misérables for 2 years in the West End, Sherbet in the 21st Anniversary production of Philip Ridley's The Fastest Clock in the Universe.

Film and TV credits include roles in The Duchess (Netflix), After Life (Netflix), Call the Midwife (BBC), and the Les Misérables movie.

Nancy runs W1 Workshops, a theatre training company offering affordable acting training.

She has recently written and directed her first short film MASTERCLASS.
