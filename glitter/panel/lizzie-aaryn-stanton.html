Lizzie Aaryn-Stanton

Lizzie Aaryn-Stanton is an actress, writer and coach based in London.

She graduated with a BA in Theatre Arts and Performance Studies from Ivy League Brown University, and an MA in Acting for Screen at the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama.

She has two best actress awards, and her debut play was accepted to The Park Theatre before lockdown.

On stage, Lizzie has played leading roles that include Nora Helmer in Henrik Ibsen's 'A Doll's House', Eliza Doolittle in 'Pygmalion', Lulu in 'Lulu', Cate in 'Blasted' and Leona Dawson in Tennessee Williams's 'Confessional'.

Her latest feature 'Book of Monsters' is available on demand, 'Faith' is due for release later this year.

Lizzie can be found in the twitter and instaverses as @LizzAarynStants and @lizzieaarynstanton.
