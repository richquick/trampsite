Kyle Rowe

Kyle Rowe is a working-class actor and producer from Manchester.

He is co-founder of Just Add Milk, a theatre company that gives unheard voices a platform.

Kyle was in the original cast of The Beast of Blue Yonder and will be performing the penultimate monologue of the sequence.

TV & Film includes roles in: Eastenders, Judy, Coronation Street, Peaky Blinders.

Theare includes: Daniel in Who Cares (Vault Festival), Michael in Under Three Moons (Box of Tricks), Mossy in Digging Deep (Just Add Milk).
