Paul Henshall

Paul Henshall is an actor and comedian.

Paul is best known for playing the roles of medical student Dean West in Holby City, and Paul in I'm With Stupid.

He's a wheelchair user and has cerebral palsy. He was the first disabled actor in the U.K. to appear as a recurring character in a recurrign drama, and the first disabled actor BADC certificate in stage combat.
