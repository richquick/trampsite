EM Williams

EM Williams is a performer, movement director, and producer specialising in physical theatre, aerial circus, and ensemble.

EM grew up in the Midlands with their Jamaican father and Irish mother.

Their studies and training began in Kent, spanned California, and culminated in London at RCSSD. Their roles have taken them to three continents (so far).

They curate Stitchin' Fiction, a London-based new writing night.
